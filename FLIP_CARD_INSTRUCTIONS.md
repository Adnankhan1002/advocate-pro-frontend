# How to Add the National Emblem Image

## Steps:

1. Save the National Emblem of India image you shared
2. Place it in: `frontend/public/images/national-emblem.jpg`
3. The image is already referenced in the ArticleFlipCard component

The flip card is now fully functional and will display the national emblem automatically once you add the image file.
